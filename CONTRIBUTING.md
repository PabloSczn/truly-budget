# Contributing

This is a public, source-available repository, but external contributions are not accepted.

## Policy

- Please do not open pull requests for code, assets, translations, documentation, or other changes.
- Feature requests and unsolicited implementation proposals may be closed without review. Though, I am always open for suggestions on my email, where you can reach out to me! sancheznarro.pablo@gmail.com

## Why

The project is intentionally maintained as a public read-only codebase. The source is visible, but the repository is not open for community contributions.
